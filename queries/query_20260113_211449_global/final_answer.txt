### Purpose and Use of H2@Home

H2@Home is a decentralized hydrogen generation system designed to produce hydrogen in a decentralized manner. Its primary use lies in showcasing innovative technical capabilities within the field of sustainable energy. By decentralizing hydrogen generation, the system contributes to advancements in energy sustainability and efficiency, addressing critical challenges in the energy sector.

### Significance and Impact

The system was developed as part of the Engineering Competition 2025, where it demonstrated its potential and technical excellence by winning first place.
